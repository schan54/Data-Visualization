VISUALIZING CLIMATE CHANGE

Mission Statement: Our goal is to provide users with unique and meaningful visualizations for understanding climate change. We believe that while one visual may provide great insight into CO2 emissions, the same visual might not be applicable or relevant for another dataset such as temperature.

We have identified data that best displays how our planet's climate is changing. Below are visualizations on carbon dioxide emissions, temperaute change and overall energy use. Our hope is that these visuals aid in understanding the severity and long term risks of global warming.
