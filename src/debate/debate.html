Defining Climate Change & the debate

Climate change is a global phenonena encapsulating changes in various weather patterns such as temperature, rainfall, sea level, and storm frequency.

The debate surrounding the legitamacy of climate change does not challenge if climate change is occuring or not, but whether or not humans are the causal affect of such a phenonena.
