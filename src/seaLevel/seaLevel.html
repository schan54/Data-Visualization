Glacial Melting

The melting of land glaciers is one of the two main causes of sea level rise. As the glaciers melt, their water drips off into the ocean, adding more volume. Areas with the biggest concern of glacial melting include Antarctica and Greenland, as they host many of the worlds land glaciers.
